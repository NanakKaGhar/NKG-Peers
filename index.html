About Nanak Ka Ghar Foundation

Nanak Ka Ghar Foundation is a distinguished nonprofit organization committed to fostering unity, compassion, and social advancement through strategic initiatives in education, healthcare, and community development.

Our foundation champions the philosophy of collective growth and peer-driven learning. NKG Peers represents our flagship educational initiative, meticulously designed to cultivate a sophisticated network that empowers students and lifelong learners to achieve excellence.
